TROONz

TROONz is a cartoon based side project of mine. TROONz is also my dance music a.k.a. Heavy use of samples mixed with electro house. Like hip-hop sensibilities meets electro house. I am also currently in the mock up stages of designing the TROONz website which will feature a shop selling all sorts of TROONz gear like: tees, shorts, caps, sweaters, toys, prints and music.
