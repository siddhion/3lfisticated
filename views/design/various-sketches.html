2010 - 2013


Architech was a dual sketch image that represented my kind of entry into programming. The top sketch is a picture of someone, me I guess, burning the midnight oil in a frenetic programming session. The bottom image is of a silly interpretation of future in-brain computing technology in which our brains are interfaced with an ultra-fast, nanoscale computer. Silly because I seriously doubt we will have or need a HUD for interfacing with in-brain computers. Rather, we will use pure thought to interface with computers. The only graphic elements we will see is video and images.

Marc Maron the Cat Wrangler was a quick sketch I made after reading Marc's Twitter bio which states he's a 'feral cat wrangler'. While I am not a feral cat wrangler, I do have two felines and relate to Marc's experience living with and interacting with cats. I also often listen to his pod cast, WTF. I uploaded the pic to Twitter and he retweeted it which was cool.

Fishy Touch is an image I drew back in 2010 which was my representation of Pixelord's track Fishy Touch.

Squirrel Sew is a cryptic drawing. The squirrel is sewing its own leg out of a loop string. Its hood has a 1 & 0 in an infinity sign. 1s and 0s are what programs are made of. Similar to the on and off nature of reality. Nature and technology merging. Or maybe nature is a form of technology for souls to inhabit. Why is it measuring the distance between its chin to its shoulder? Is it even doing that? And look its eye is a pin hole. Its holding a needle. Did he stab itself a pupil? Or did the needle come from its pupil eye hole? Look at the location of the needle. Is the needle it's penis? Eye sex? I'll stop now.
