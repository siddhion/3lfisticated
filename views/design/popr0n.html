Popr0n

3ambi

2012


I was approached by an underground painter named 3ambi to come up with some concept designs for a series of pornographic inspired pop art paintings he wanted his team of painters to execute. sHe is big into outsourcing everything sHe does which is not a big deal since sHe doesn't consider hiermself an artist in particular, rather - an artistically inclined entrepreneur.

Anyways, I came up with these pieces which I name Popr0n, the merger between 'Pop' and 'Pr0n'. For those who don't know, 'pr0n' means pornography viewed online or on a computer which is where I got all my source images I used for inspiration.
