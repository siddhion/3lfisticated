Lounge Coffee

Lemy Leopard x Laëtitia Dana

2012


Lemy Leopard told me about his collaboration with Laëtitia Dana named Lounge Coffee in late 2012. Set to be a self titled EP, Lounge Coffee would be a deep vocal house record. Since it was a digital release only, just the front cover art needed to be designed and I got on it right away.

Lemy and Laëtitia wanted the cover art to be of a loungy vibe with earthy, coffee tones but it also had to reflect the nature of dance and house music. The background characters dancing were drawing in a brush that looked like a stylized coffee stain. Each character is sending energy to each other, connecting each other to make one circuit.
