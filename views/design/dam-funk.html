Dam-Funk & Stones Throw Records

2012


I first heard Dam-Funk's music on Myspace in early 2009 and immediately fell in love with it so I did a logo for him out of my appreciation. He liked it and later that year in the summer, he approached me to do a redesign of the logo for his upcoming debut LP release on Stones Throw called Toeachizown. The final logo was taken and used on the album cover which was first released as a 5 LP boxed vinyl set, then on each of the 5 individual vinyl volumes sold separately and lastly on the full-length CD.

Later in 2012 I reconnected with Dam-Funk and slightly redesigned Dam-Funk's Toeachizown logo to color and texture match the first 2009 fan logo I did for him. That original logo had an L.A. sunset image background. Stones Throw took my new redesign and gave it to Stussy Japan to make into a t-shirt and flier to promote Dam-Funk's 2012 Japan tour => STUSSY Ã— STONES THROW : DAM-FUNK JAPAN TOUR TEE.

Additionally Dam-Funk asked if I still had one of the concept designs I made for his 2009 logo. It was a simple D and F side by side. He wanted to use it as a t-shirt design which is now available in his online store.
