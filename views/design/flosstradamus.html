Flosstradamus PLURNT Logo

Flosstradamus

2013


I got in touch with Flosstradamus showing them my 3Dification of the Fool's Gold Records logo. I basically turned their 2D logo (a gold bar with Fool's Gold on it) into a shiny, realistic, 3D gold bar and created a product concept around it called Fool's Gold Mint.

The PLURT logo took me a while. In the concept phase I was considering making the logo in a Baroque-esque / Late Renaisance design style with very complicated, flowery, decorative, psychedlic swirls. I tried to make it in my 3D software and realized it would take me forever so I went with a solid, minimalistic and clean design. Made it in super shiny flossed out gold. Included PLURNT on the triangle's base.

The idea was to deconstruct the name Flosstradamus and create an image which represented that. So we have Floss which basically means to show off things that appear to be of high value. That is why I chose the marble surface, gold framed embossed velvet pillow (like that of a nations Crown Jewels) and the gold PLURNT icon resting upon it.

I assumed the rest of Flosstradamus's name to have come from Nostradamus who was the famous prophet who wrote the famous book called Les Propheties (The Prophecies). I chose to emboss that book title across the top of the velvet cushion in the same font used in its original printing.
