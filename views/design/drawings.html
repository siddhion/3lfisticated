Cloudsick

Personal

2010


I started to experiment with fast sketching cartoons. I would rapidly draw each character in light lines with out analyzing the lines. It would take about 15 to 30 seconds per character. No editing. No erasing. Then I would render the meticulous details, like shadows, wounds and skin folds, over this quick sketch. That ultra detailed rendering process would take hours. The outcome were a group of grotesque, fluffy, damaged yet innocent characters. These are paradoxical beings because innocence and corruption combine almost all of them. There is the one that is alone and frustrated. I suppose that one demonstrates the process of the innocence being attacked by the corruptive.
