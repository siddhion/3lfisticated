Dam-Funk & Stones Throw Records

2012


I first heard Dam-Funk's music on Myspace in early 2009 and immediately fell in love with it. It brought back memories of my childhood instantly. I did a logo for him out of my appreciation and he liked it. Later that year in the summer, he approached me to do a redesign of the logo for his upcoming debut LP release on Stones Throw called Toeachizown. The final logo was taken and used on the album which was first released as a 5 LP boxed vinyl set, then on each of the 5 individual vinyl volumes sold separately and lastly on the full-length CD.

Later in 2012 I slightly redesigned Dam-Funk's Toeachizown logo to color and texture match the first 2009 fan logo I did for him. Stones Throw took my new redesign and gave it to Stussy Japan to make into a t-shirt and flier to promote Dam-Funk's 2012 Japan tour => STUSSY Ã— STONES THROW : DAM-FUNK JAPAN TOUR TEE.

Additionally Damon asked if I still had one of the concept designs I made for his 2009 logo. It was a simple D and F side by side. He wanted to use it as a t-shirt design which is now available in his online store.
