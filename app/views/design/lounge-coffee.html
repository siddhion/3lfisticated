Lounge Coffee

Lemy Leopard x Laëtitia Dana

2012


Lemy Leopard told me about his collaboration with Laëtitia Dana named Lounge Coffee in late 2012. Set to be a self titled EP, Lounge Coffee would be a deep vocal house record. Since it was a digital release only, just the front cover art needed to be designed and I got on it right away. Lemy and Laëtitia wanted the cover art to be of a loungy vibe with earthy, coffee tones yet it also had to reflect dance and house music. Mission successful!
