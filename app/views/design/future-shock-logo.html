Future Shock Logo

Ray Gun from Future Shock

2012


Ray Gun from the band Future Shock contacted me after seeing my 2012 redesign of Dam-Funk's logo and wanted me to work on their logo. Unfortunately Ray Gun and Future Shock seemed to disappear from the Internet all together. Oh well! I still think the final design and the earlier prototypes are worth representing on this here site.
