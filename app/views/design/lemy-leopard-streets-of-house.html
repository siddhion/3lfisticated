Lemy Leopard : Streets of House

Lemy Leopard

2012


Lemy Leopard contacted me after he saw my 2012 redesign of Dam-Funk's logo via a retweet from Dam-Funk and wanted me to design the cover art for his upcoming EP, Streets of House. I liked the music Lemy was making and I got to work.

We both agreed to make it minimal and simple. The grid background was inspired by the original Streets Of Rage Genesis game box art. The logo also was directly inspired by the original game logo. Lemy wanted it to have a laid back L.A. sunset vibe so I Photoshopped multiple /images of a night time L.A. together, added a surreal gradient sunset sky and faded the sky into the background grid. The back cover was just a simple reflection of the front cover styling - a simple grid and most lettering fitting perfectly in each box.

Streets of House is Lemy's second EP on the French house music label, Qalomota Records.
