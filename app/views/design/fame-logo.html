F.A.M.E. Logo

Max Kane of F.A.M.E. (Fresh Analog Music Experience)

2009


F.A.M.E. a.k.a. Fresh Analog Music Experience is a live electronic funk band out of L.A., CA. consisting of Max Kane, Teeko and Malaguti. Producer, DJ and performer Max Kane, one third of F.A.M.E., contacted me to do a logo for the band after seeing the logo I designed for Dam Funk's 2009 Stones Throw release, Toeachizown. After playing around with a few basic concepts I finally arrived at a minimal, futuristic logo that appeared on their self released album TruthPics
